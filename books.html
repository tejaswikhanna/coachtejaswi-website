Embracing Failure

When things fall apart, you rise stronger

Embracing Failure reflects on the fragile, honest moments when life seems to crack. Using personal stories, practical exercises, and the gentle philosophy of kintsugi, this book shows how brokenness can become a map back to yourself — clearer, kinder, and more whole.
Failure
We’ve all met it — in exams, in careers, in love, in dreams that never took flight. But what if failure was never your enemy at all? What if it was your most patient teacher, quietly shaping the person you’re meant to become?
In Embracing Failure, Tejaswi Khanna takes readers on a reflective journey through stories, parables, and gentle practices that reframe how we see our stumbles. Through the eyes of two seekers — Ishaan and Dev — and through tales of mountains, rivers, broken vases, and phoenixes, this book becomes both mirror and map for anyone who’s ever fallen and wondered if they could rise again.
Each chapter ends with Reflection Nuggets and Practical Exercises that turn philosophy into experience — inviting you not to rush through, but to walk with the book, to let it breathe with you. Written with simplicity and depth, Embracing Failure is less a self-help manual and more a spiritual companion — reminding us that every fall holds a lesson, every crack lets the light in, and that the teacher we seek often hides in the very places we resist.

Awaken Each Day

365 Reflections for the Soul

Awaken Each Day is a daily companion for presence — short reflections, tiny practices, and gentle prompts designed to help you start each day with curiosity and calm. A book to read a page at a time, and return to again and again. A daily book of reflection and mindfulness, blending ancient wisdom with modern life. Short reflections to pause, breathe, and reconnect — a one-year companion for presence. very morning carries a whisper — a chance to begin again. Awaken Each Day is not just a book to read, but a companion to return to — a space to pause, breathe, and realign with what truly matters.
Through 365 reflections, Tejaswi Khanna invites you to explore the subtle corners of the soul — where fear softens into courage, chaos turns into clarity, and ordinary moments reveal quiet grace. Each page offers a gentle awakening — a reminder that growth is not found in grand leaps, but in small, conscious steps taken every day.
Whether you begin your day with it or close your night in its silence, Awaken Each Day will guide you to live with more awareness, calm, and compassion — one reflection at a time.
