About Tejaswi

Writer • Coach • Storyteller

Welcome. I'm Tejaswi Khanna — a writer who believes that our most broken moments often become our most beautiful teachers.

Through my books, essays, and reflections, I explore the quiet spaces between failure and growth, between falling apart and coming back together. My work draws from personal stories, ancient wisdom, and the simple truth that we all carry cracks — and that those cracks are where the light gets in.

Whether you're navigating a difficult transition, seeking more presence in your daily life, or simply looking for words that feel like a conversation with a friend — I hope you'll find something here that resonates.

My Philosophy

My approach is rooted in three core beliefs that guide everything I write and share.

🌱

Growth Through Failure

Failure isn't the opposite of success — it's the pathway to it. Every stumble carries a lesson, every broken moment holds wisdom.

🧘

Presence Over Perfection

True transformation happens not through grand gestures, but through small, conscious steps taken with awareness and compassion.

✨

Kintsugi Spirit

Like the Japanese art of repairing broken pottery with gold, our cracks and scars can become our most beautiful features.

My Journey

The Beginning

My journey into writing and coaching began with my own encounters with failure — moments when life didn't go as planned, and I had to find new ways forward.

Embracing Failure

My first book, Embracing Failure, emerged from years of reflection on how our broken moments shape us. It's a collection of stories, practices, and gentle wisdom for anyone navigating difficult transitions.

Daily Reflections

My upcoming book, Awaken Each Day, offers 365 daily reflections — a companion for presence, calm, and mindful living.

Today

I continue to write, reflect, and share — creating spaces for authentic conversation about failure, growth, and the courage to keep showing up.
